## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a **Software Engineer** focused on **software engineering and architecture**, building scalable, resilient, and domain-driven enterprise solutions.

My work sits at the intersection of enterprise systems, data integration, and intelligent automation, with hands-on experience in:

- **Software architecture** for distributed systems and integrations between legacy and modern platforms  
- **Data migration** (mapping, cleansing, loading, reconciliation, and quality governance)  
- **Messaging and asynchronous integration** for decoupling, reliability, and scale  
- **AI Agents and RAG pipelines** applied to business workflows and operational automation  
- **CRM engineering**, including **modeling and customization with Odoo** to build and evolve commercial processes  
- **Backend and platform engineering** with Java, Python, and TypeScript, running on Docker, Kubernetes, Linux, AWS, and OCI

I focus on end-to-end delivery: from architecture design to production environments, with strong attention to observability, performance, and long-term maintainability.
